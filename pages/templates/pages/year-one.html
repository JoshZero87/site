Local Groups

Our Revolution is made up of 401 local groups all across the world. With groups in 49 states and Puerto Rico as well as international groups in Austria, France and Canada, the poltical revolution continues under the leadership of local leaders all across the globe.

Our Revolution groups have elected candidates across the nation, passed ballot initiatives, reorganized their local parties, held candidate trainings and advocated for issues that are driving our nation forward - and we’re just getting started.

Our Revolution groups are transforming the Democratic Party.

Over the course of six months, Our Revolution has been involved in recruiting candidates for, and driving voter turnout in, Democratic Party Reorganization in 18 states and 20 Democrats Abroad member countries.

Over 16,000 people have been encouraged to run for party office as a result of our outreach. Already, we have allies holding State and County Chair and other executive positions across the country, working to transform the Democratic Party into a party that truly reflects and represents the people in each and every community through the U.S.

Progressive Issues

Our Revolution groups have been fighting for progressive issues all across the country - and we’ve been winning.

Right now, a mass coalition of progressive organizations are leading the Summer for Progress, an effort to get House Democrats to endorse the People’s Platform – 8 bills that say definitively people over profit.

Over the past year, citizen leaders have organized sit-ins and rallies across the country to save tha ACA and we’ve pushed for sanctuary community policies all around the country. We’ve fought pipelines and stopped the Trans-Pacific Partnership, and successfully petitioned for the freedom of Oscar Lopez Rivera.

Funded by the people.

Our Revolution is completey funded by individual donations, proving that organizations and candidates don’t have to rely on huge corporate contributions to thrive.

Our Revolution's founding supporters made more than 140,000 individual contributions during the organization's first six months, and more than 58,000 contributions during the first quarter of 2017.
