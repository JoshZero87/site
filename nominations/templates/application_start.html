Start a new endorsement application

All Our Revolution endorsements are based on the input from those that live in the community. An Our Revolution endorsement begins with a recommendation from one out of the hundreds of official Our Revolution local groups.

Official local groups have gone through our group onboarding process, signed our Memorandum of Understanding, and received a Group ID. To find or form a local group in your area, please visit our map.

To submit a new endorsement application, you'll need some basic information about your group, contact information for one group representative, and information about the candidate or ballot initiative in question.
